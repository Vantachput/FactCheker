FactChecker — AI-Powered Fake News Detector
===========================================

An intelligent Telegram bot for detecting disinformation in social media.
The bot verifies news authenticity using a hybrid AI architecture:
web search (Serper + GPT RAG pipeline), Perplexity AI with deep-research
mode, and fine-tuned models (Llama 3.1, GPT-4o-mini).
